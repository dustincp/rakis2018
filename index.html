About Rakis

Rakis has been Colorado's quintessential Amtgard event for more than 20 years. Foam fighters, artisans, entertainers, and their friends and families come together every Summer to help us throw an amazing event. Having built its reputation on creative battlegames, great meals, exciting Jugging, high-end arts and sciences, and crafty roleplaying, this year's Rakis is on course to be spectacular. I know we say that every year, but we can't help it. We've also moved away from the eastern plains and will be in the mountains once again, about 40 minutes from Colorado Springs.

We've got a full schedule for 2018's Back Into the Woods Rakis. Whether you're a fighter, crafter, merchant, entertainer, or partier, Rakis should be on your Summer schedule.

Games and Roleplay

Rakis offers battlegames catering to those of you who want large scale action as well as a chance to fight with your friends. In the mornings we're hosting small team battlegames. Games where you can get your squad together for some high quality competitive gaming. The afternoons and evening are reserved for more robust thematic battlegames.

Phoenix Ball is also coming to Rakis. Get your teams ready for an exciting game style built to let you show off your tactics and team play.

Food, Crafting, and Entertainment

Delicious meals will be provided Friday and Saturday night. For those interested in fancier fare, we have 20 spots available for our King's Feast Saturday night. For just $25 you'll be treated to a meal of tomato basil soup, bread baskets, cheese and fruit trays, smoked quail, garlic roasted carrots, roasted onions & potatoes in gravy, and bee sting cake.

Are you a crafter? Enter our cross-discipline single item Best-of-the-Best Arts and Sciences tournament and compete against top talent from multiple kingdoms in a bracketed showdown for some fantastic bragging rights.

For those of you looking to pick up some new skills, we'll also be hosting four different arts & sciences seminars on belt weaving, fairy wing construction, plaster carving, and crochet pouches.

Need a break from night ditching? A stroll through camp will quickly land you at a lively party, some interesting games, or an entertaining bardic.

Best of the Best A&S Tournament

Every year at Rakis, top artisans enter a single item in our bracketed Arts & Sciences tournament. Entrants present or perform directly to the panel of judges and receive immediate feedback about their entry. After the judges have seen everything, the entries get sorted into a double-elimination tournament bracket where the judges have to look at each set of entries to determine what moves on to become the Best of the Best.
