About Rakis

Iä! Iä! The stars align. Madness abounds. The Great Old Ones stir. What little sanity you've managed to hold onto slips away. You succumb to that which man was not meant to know.

Rakis has been Colorado's quintessential Amtgard event for more than 20 years. Foam fighters, artisans, entertainers, and their friends and families come together every Summer to help us throw an amazing event. Having built its reputation on creative battlegames, great meals, exciting Jugging, high-end arts and sciences, and crafty roleplaying, this year's Rakis is on course to be spectacular. I know we say that every year, but we can't help it.

We've got a full schedule for 2017's Cthulhu mythos themed Rakis. Whether you're a fighter, crafter, merchant, entertainer, or partier, Rakis should be on your Summer schedule.

Games and Roleplay

Plenty of ditching on our lit field and a full schedule of battlegames provide ample venues for swordplay and spellcraft.

For those seeking adventure less common, perhaps membership in the Hermetic Order of the Silver Twilight or a trip through the Dreamlands, Rakis also has a set of games more focused on roleplaying. If you've attended before and are familar with our usual Roleplay Tavern, we're upgrading those events and putting them directly on the schedule. This year features a number of small-team quests that will take a bit of cunning and may give you an advantage in the larger battlegames.

We'll also be hosting another Rakis staple, a Jugging tournament. Gather your team and equipment for some brutal combat to place a dog skull on a spike. If that doesn't sound like a dark ritual, I don't know what does.

Want another outlet for mystical medieval murder mischief? If you ever find yourself saying, "Oh boy, here I go killing again," then our Assassin's Game is for you. Spend your time between other events sneaking up on your friends and stabbing them.

Food, Crafting, and Entertainment

Feasts will be provided Friday and Saturday night. The menus aren't finalized yet, but we assure you they won't disappoint. Some donation feasts will also be provided for other meals.

Are you a crafter? Enter our cross-discipline single item Best-of-the-Best Arts and Sciences tournament and compete against top talent from multiple kingdoms in a bracketed showdown for some fantastic bragging rights.

Need a break from night ditching? A stroll through camp will quickly land you at a lively party, some interesting games, or an entertaining bardic. While these are generally up to the attendees to coordinate, there will be an official event party Friday night.

Best of the Best A&S Tournament

Every year at Rakis, top artisans enter a single item in our bracketed Arts & Sciences tournament. Entrants present or perform directly to the panel of judges and receive immediate feedback about their entry. After the judges have seen everything, the entries get sorted into a double-elimination tournament bracket where the judges have to look at each set of entries to determine what moves on to become the Best of the Best.

Merchants

Bring a little extra coin and visit our wonderful merchants.



The Prestige

The Prestige is your one stop shoppe for all things LARP related, presented by Sutra Bahuas and Erra of Midnight Sun. You'll find great manufactured/handmade clothing, leather products, baubles, trinkets, LARP weapons and shields, and a slew of of other wonderful curios!

GoodBasicGarb

GoodBasicgarb, provides quality artisanship at a fair price from such name brands as Chase the Myth, Gorg the Blacksmith, Anubis's Defense, Lady Shore, Force of Giants, Gentleman JoDon Kilts and more.

Songtailor Stitchery

Specializing in custom garb and accessories. You will find affordable new tunics, fun sword covers, jewelry, and more.

Illeria's Trades

Hand crafted flowered hair pieces, and handmade wire-wrapped stone necklaces. A must for any wishing to look their best!
