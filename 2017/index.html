About Rakis

Iä! Iä! The stars align. Madness abounds. The Great Old Ones stir. What little sanity you've managed to hold onto slips away. You succumb to that which man was not meant to know.

Rakis has been Colorado's quintessential Amtgard event for more than 20 years. Foam fighters, artisans, entertainers, and their friends and families come together every Summer to help us throw an amazing event. Having built its reputation on creative battlegames, great meals, exciting Jugging, high-end arts and sciences, and crafty roleplaying, this year's Rakis is on course to be spectacular. I know we say that every year, but we can't help it.

We've got a full schedule for 2017's Cthulhu mythos themed Rakis. Whether you're a fighter, crafter, merchant, entertainer, or partier, Rakis should be on your Summer schedule.

Games and Roleplay

Plenty of ditching on our lit field and a full schedule of battlegames provide ample venues for swordplay and spellcraft.

For those seeking adventure less common, perhaps membership in the Hermetic Order of the Silver Twilight or a trip through the Dreamlands, Rakis also has a set of games more focused on roleplaying. If you've attended before and are familar with our usual Roleplay Tavern, we're upgrading those events and putting them directly on the schedule. This year features a number of small-team quests that will take a bit of cunning and may give you an advantage in the larger battlegames.

We'll also be hosting another Rakis staple, a Jugging tournament. Gather your team and equipment for some brutal combat to place a dog skull on a spike. If that doesn't sound like a dark ritual, I don't know what does.

Want another outlet for mystical medieval murder mischief? If you ever find yourself saying, "Oh boy, here I go killing again," then our Assassin's Game is for you. Spend your time between other events sneaking up on your friends and stabbing them.

Food, Crafting, and Entertainment

Feasts will be provided Friday and Saturday night. The menus aren't finalized yet, but we assure you they won't disappoint. Some donation feasts will also be provided for other meals.

Are you a crafter? Enter our cross-discipline single item Best-of-the-Best Arts and Sciences tournament and compete against top talent from multiple kingdoms in a bracketed showdown for some fantastic bragging rights.

Need a break from night ditching? A stroll through camp will quickly land you at a lively party, some interesting games, or an entertaining bardic. While these are generally up to the attendees to coordinate, there will be an official event party Friday night.

Orientation Day at Miskatonic University - Militia Battlegame

Famous for its archive of occult book, the library at Miskatonic University holds one of the few genuine copies of the "Mad Arab" Abdul Alhazred’s Necronomicon. Finding a copy won’t be easy between magical wards that hide it and those willing to kill to find it before you can. Whoever finds this horrible book has the potential to unlock powerful secrets useful for aiding or combating the Great Old Ones.

This will be a two team milita style game that takes place in the Miskatonic University library (represented by a large maze). Scattered throughout the library are research stations where players can search for signs of the Necronomicon. Players will need to take control of research stations by marking them for their team. Players can take control of research stations already controlled by the opposing team. Periodically, reeves will award points to the teams for each research station they control at that time. Once time is up, the reeves will total points to determine a winning team.

A Small Sacrifice for Knowledge - Full Class Battlegame

After a brutal search, the Necronomicon has been located. Finding it has released a magical energy that calls forth many who seek its terrible knowledge. Groups of cultists, researchers, and would-be heroes have gathered to glean its contents.

Best of the Best A&S Tournament

Every year at Rakis, top artisans enter a single item in our bracketed Arts & Sciences tournament. Entrants present or perform directly to the panel of judges and receive immediate feedback about their entry. After the judges have seen everything, the entries get sorted into a double-elimination tournament bracket where the judges have to look at each set of entries to determine what moves on to become the Best of the Best.

11:00 AM
Legends of the Sunken Temple or R’lyeh Renovation - Full Class Battlegame

Elder Gods need worshippers. What better way to show your support than to erect effigies? This sunken temple contains the remains of tributes to the Great Old Ones from an ancient time. As usual, you aren’t the only one here begging Yog-Senpai to notice you.

This is a full class game with two teams competing to see who can construct a set of totems first. The field will be littered with colored totem pieces, which the teams will have to gather and assemble. A completed totem consists of a head, body, and tail - each of a different color. The first team with two completed totems wins.

After Best of the Best
Old Ones and Acolytes A&S

After Friday's Best of the Best, come cool off in the Quonset hut! A&S Anonymous invites Old Ones and Acolytes alike to reap the benefits of each others' knowledge, company, and overflowing craft closets.

Old Ones: Bring fabric you'd like to see on a person instead of a shelf, supplies and tools that deserve dusting off, and all those teas you were never going to drink anyway.

Acolytes: New the the arts, on a tight budget, or looking to branch out? Maybe you just want to know what the big deal is about Oolong. Pull up an altar, let's get crafty.

2:30 PM
The Blind Idiot God’s Totally Sane Full Class Battlegame Tournament

Think your group is the best when magic, armor, and projectiles are in play? Prove it on the field and gain favor with ancient deities, or die and be sacrificed to Azathoth’s flopping horde of mindless dancers, drummers, and flute players.

Gather a team of up to 6 players to compete in this battlegame tournament. There will be 3 fields, each with a different game that teams will use in a round-robin tournament. Depending on the number of teams that enter, we’ll run either a full or limited round robin tournament with a set of finals to determine the overall winner. Fields will be:

King of the Hill - The team that spends the most time in control of the hill wins.

Flag Capture - Multiple flags will be scattered across the field. The team with the most of their opponent’s flags wins.

Push-Pull - Each team must push a single object along a path towards their opponent’s base. The team with the object closest to their base at the end of the game loses.

The Rakis Horror - Full Class Battlegame

A frightening abomination writhes among a circle of stone pillars, attempting to gain purchase in this world. Newly born, the young spawn of an Elder God needs to be fed energy until it can feed itself on the minds of others. Right now is the weakest it will ever be. Using the knowledge gained from the Necronomicon, you can choose to feed it power or sever its connection to our reality.

This is a two team full class game. One team will attempt to empower a new Elder God by activating 8 pillars of power, while the other team will attempt to weaken it by deactivating those same pillars. The state of each pillar is determined by a set of flags that can be raised and lowered by the players. A multi-player monster, the new Elder God, will follow a path that moves between the pillars and destroy everything it can - making the job difficult for both teams. At set intervals, reeves will assess the state of each pillar and award points to the teams accordingly. The team with the most points wins.

Merchants

Bring a little extra coin and visit our wonderful merchants.



The Prestige

The Prestige is your one stop shoppe for all things LARP related, presented by Sutra Bahuas and Erra of Midnight Sun. You'll find great manufactured/handmade clothing, leather products, baubles, trinkets, LARP weapons and shields, and a slew of of other wonderful curios!

GoodBasicGarb

GoodBasicgarb, provides quality artisanship at a fair price from such name brands as Chase the Myth, Gorg the Blacksmith, Anubis's Defense, Lady Shore, Force of Giants, Gentleman JoDon Kilts and more.

Songtailor Stitchery

Specializing in custom garb and accessories. You will find affordable new tunics, fun sword covers, jewelry, and more.

Illeria's Trades

Hand crafted flowered hair pieces, and handmade wire-wrapped stone necklaces. A must for any wishing to look their best!

Madussa's Layers

Coming from the far off land of Missouri, offering garb, feastware, and other medieval shinies for all your larping wants and needs. One-of-a-kind pieces you won't find elsewhere at very affordable prices. Also carries pouches, belts and other assorted leather goodies.

Dragon Wyck Embroidery

Specializing in embroidered items such as dice bags, patches, and belt favors, Dragon Wyck Embroidery can also can make shield covers and sword covers. If you have a image you would like to see turned into embroidery Dragon Wyck Embroidery might be able to help.
