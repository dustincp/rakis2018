About Rakis

Romans! The time has come once against to test your might on the field of honor. Come best your opponents by strength or by cunning and stand proud, awash in glory.

Rakis has been Colorado's quintessential Amtgard event for more than 20 years. Foam fighters, artisans, entertainers, and their friends and families come together every Summer to help us throw an amazing event. Having built its reputation on creative battlegames, great meals, exciting Jugging, high-end arts and sciences, and a crafty roleplaying tavern, this year's Rakis has a lot to live up to.

Always looking to step up from the previous year, we've got a full schedule for 2016's Roman themed Rakis. Whether you're a fighter, crafter, bard, or partier, Rakis should be on your Summer schedule.

Games and Roleplay

Plenty of ditching on our lit field, battlegames, Jugging, and an archery tournament should go a long way towards sating anyone's lust for blood and prestige.

For those looking for a bit more intrigue and guile, our RP Tavern will have quests available to undertake for the duration of the event as well as an Assassin's Game. Your skills could earn you riches or infamy.

Are you a crafter? Enter our cross-discipline single item Best-of-the-Best Arts and Sciences tournament and compete against top talent from multiple kingdoms in a bracketed showdown for some fantastic bragging rights.

Food and Entertainment

A full compliment of meals will be provided throughout the event by our staff and generous volunteers, but you're encouraged to bring your own libations if you need to offer any tributes during the event.

Need a break from night ditching? A stroll through camp will quickly land you at a lively party, some interesting games, or an entertaining bardic. This is one area where we leave it to the attendees to bring their own preferred form of fun to share. They have never disappointed.

RP Tavern Opens

A nexus for players new and old that are interested in the finer details of our world, all should make a point to visit. Become famous for your heroics, earn medallions for your deeds, amaze others with your cunning, or secure your position as the master of assassins. The RP Tavern runs for the duration of the event and acts as a starting point for questing and the assassin's game. Stop by often to get involved in the latest adventures.

11:00 AM
Militia Battlegame - The Aeneid

Join us in helping Aeneas flee from the Greeks so he can found Rome. This battlegame will consist of two teams on two large ships each trying to burn the other down to send them to a watery grave. But beware of the monster Charybdis who plans on murdering any sailor it can get its tentacles on!

12:00 PM
Lunch

Seattle style sausage on a bun, donated by the Wolves of Cernunnos.

3:00 PM
Full Class Battlegame - Beware the Ides of March

Let’s kill Caesar! Enter the halls of the great Roman Senate for a battlegame that will consist of two teams: the Roman military loyal to Caesar who wishes to protect their beloved and glorious leader and the Roman Senators who wish to assassinate the tyrant that is destroying our great Roman Republic. Caesar has his own ideas however and refuses to be commanded by anyone, but at least he is immune to all forms of attacks save those special game item daggers blessed by Jupiter himself. Let’s hope the Senators can stab him enough times to end his reign!

6:00 PM
Dinner

Italian sausage sandwiches with bake penn and sauteed balsamic vegetables donated by Wu Tang.

10:00 AM
Best of the Best A&S Tournament

Every year at Rakis, top artisans enter a single item in our bracketed Arts & Sciences tournament. Entrants present or perform directly to the panel of judges and receive immediate feedback about their entry. After the judges have seen everything, the entries get sorted into a double-elimination tournament bracket where the judges have to look at each set of entries to determine what moves on to become the Best of the Best.

11:00 AM
Full Class Battlegame - Hannibal Crossing the Alps

Elephants are awesome aren’t they? Even better than elephants are War Elephants! Too bad they have to be herded through the snowy, narrow, death-trap trails of the Alps before they become useful. This battlegame is a move heavy object game where the objects are living NPC elephants. Because of the stress of the snow, the elephants are docile unless attacked. Can the Carthaginian army get the elephants out of the Alps before the Romans slaughter them all? Tune in at Rakis to find out.

12:00 PM
Lunch

Awaiting your hungry bellies will be an offering of red chili, laboriously prepared for both carnivore and herbivore alike, and the convenient addition of drop biscuits to sop up the juices.

3:00 PM
Full Class Battlegame - Battle for Hadrian’s Wall

The barbarians are at the gates… Literally! Come join us at Hadrian’s Wall to defend the ever expanding Roman Empire from the unwashed masses of the uncivilized north. Or perhaps you would prefer to be a Pict come to smash in some pompous Roman skulls? If so, your team will need to be clever enough to figure out how to raise the gate and claim the flag in the milecastle! The team that manages it the fastest will win the day.

Merchants

Bring a little extra coin and visit our wonderful merchants.



The Prestige

The Prestige is your one stop shoppe for all things LARP related, presented by Sutra Bahuas and Erra of Midnight Sun. You'll find great manufactured/handmade clothing, leather products, baubles, trinkets, LARP weapons and shields, and a slew of of other wonderful curios!

GoodBasicGarb and ChaseTheMyth

GBG aims to create and sell durable, dependable garb for live medieval gaming; meant to be a sound foundation on which to build your individual persona. Also included under Chasethemyth/Goodbasicgarb's roof are items from Callir Leather and Forged Foam!

Illeria's Trades
